The invisible fatigue insight

Most people do not realize how tired their eyes are. They adapt, push through, and normalize the strain — until it shows up as bad sleep, lost focus, and lower daily output. Clinics can name this invisible problem, prescribe a simple recovery ritual, and watch patients come back for more. That loop — name it, prescribe it, see the reorder — is the engine behind everything Umay does.

Product and regulatory posture

Umay Rest is a consumer wellness device: Health Canada consumer safety approved, IEC 60335-1 certified. The company uses a four-tier claims ladder with a five-gate evidence system. In plain terms: we say only what the evidence supports, we track what we are allowed to say, and we have a formal process to unlock stronger claims as evidence grows (see Appendix A). This protects regulatory flexibility and keeps trust with ODs intact.

RestScan is a browser-based, webcam-powered ocular fatigue and stress assessment tool in development. It runs 100% locally — no video stored, no facial data transmitted. It will add measurement and personalization to the clinic visit, but it is not required for near-term growth. Target: clinic-ready ~12 months post-close (see Appendix F for privacy architecture, Appendix O for roadmap).

Three strategic layers

1. Today — Ocular Recovery Platform: Clinically informed, at-home recovery for dry eye symptoms, digital strain, and ocular fatigue. Clinics prescribe it. Patients use it. Both buy again.

2. Next — Closed-Loop Measurement (~12 months post-close): RestScan adds an objective signal to the clinic visit — turning a subjective recommendation into a data-backed prescription moment.

3. Future — Autonomic Recovery / Neuro-Ocular Health: Pursued only when evidence and adoption justify it. Not a distraction; a gated option.

Roadmap in one line: Scale the clinic-led platform first. Introduce RestScan after clinical validation. Extend into autonomic health only when the data earns it.

2. Strategic Thesis and Category Positioning

Why does this category exist, and why is Umay the one to own it? The answer shapes how investors should size the opportunity.

Umay’s strategy starts from the most credible edge: optometric clinical authority paired with a product that already works in market. The company does not “sell wellness into clinics.” It exports clinical credibility into wellness — using clinics as the trust engine, then widening access as logistics and capacity catch up.

The foundational insight: invisible ocular fatigue. Patients cannot accurately gauge how strained their eyes are. The damage shows up indirectly — worse sleep, less focus, lower output. Clinics are not just buying a device. They are buying a structured answer to a problem that has never had measurable feedback before. This insight drives product design, clinic adoption, and the entire platform expansion path.

2.1 Category Framing

Category clarity matters because it changes how investors size the opportunity. Umay should be read as two things:

  • Ocular Recovery Platform (primary): Rest is an at-home recovery system for eye comfort and relaxation. It fits into a habit window patients already have (10 minutes before bed), which drives high adherence and low returns. Clinics are repeatable distribution nodes — one relationship, many patient conversions (see Appendix K).
  • Closed-Loop Measurement (architectural layer): RestScan adds an objective measure at the start of the care journey. It turns a subjective “your eyes seem dry” into a data-backed prescription moment — without claiming diagnosis. This strengthens conversion, reinforces adherence, and builds a compounding data asset (see Appendix O).

Category framing drives the execution roadmap (see Section 7).

2.2 Tone: Quietly Confident, Clinically Grounded

Umay is positioned as clinically adjacent wellness: OD-led, science-backed, compliance-aware. Ambition shows up in defensible execution — patents, reorder data, clinic adoption — not hype. 8. Institutional Readiness

Regulatory, privacy, insurance, and legal — are the foundations solid? Yes. Here is the proof.

8.1 Claims Governance

Regulatory discipline protects the staged roadmap: we scale what is proven, and introduce new claims only when evidence supports them (see Section 7).

Umay’s claims governance is an operating system, not a document on a shelf. Four-tier claims ladder. Five-gate evidence graduation. Partner compliance language that scales beyond founder oversight. In practice: Tier 1 claims (market size, general comfort language) can be used freely. Rest device telemetry is limited to anonymized usage metadata (session duration, mode, temperature). Customer PII is standard e-commerce data handled by Shopify. Before RestScan moves beyond pilots, Umay will update its privacy policy and add explicit camera consent flows. (See Appendix F.)

8.4 Material Agreements

Lean agreement footprint. All IP assigned to the company. No exclusivity traps. No change-of-control clauses.
