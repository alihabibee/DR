Key Features

3-statement integration: Income statement flows to cash flow flows to balance sheet. All 18 months balance to the dollar, validated by automated gates.

Scenario architecture: Single-cell toggle switches between Bear, Base, and Bull assumptions across all tabs simultaneously. Investor can stress-test any scenario.

Rolling close support: Fundraise tranches modeled as variable inputs — update as checks come in, cash waterfall adjusts automatically.

Actuals tracking: Every calculation sheet has actuals-vs-forecast rows ready for monthly population during the raise period.

Clinic cohort engine: Revenue builds from individual clinic activation quarters, not top-down estimates. Reorder decay, dormant reactivation, and new activation rates drive the flywheel.

Yellow-highlighted cells indicate assumptions that will upgrade when primary data arrives (clinic order history export, Aztech batch pricing, actual payment terms). The model is built to improve incrementally as data flows in.
