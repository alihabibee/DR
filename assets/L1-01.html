Stage 0
The Origin Story

Your Brain Stops Feeling Tired.
Your Body Doesn't Stop Being Impaired.

After 3-4 days of sleep restriction, subjective sleepiness plateausyou stop feeling worse. But objective cognitive impairment keeps accumulating. By day 14, you're as impaired as someone who hasn't slept in two days, while reporting you feel "only slightly sleepy."
