Why It Works as a Sales Tool

The #1 patient objection at $500+ is purchase anxiety: "What if it doesn't work for me?" The 100-night trial eliminates this entirely. It transfers risk from patient to manufacturer — the same approach that built Casper ($575M revenue) and Warby Parker ($620M revenue).

Staff script: "It comes with a 100-night risk-free trial — so you can try it with confidence."

Why Return Rate Is Exceptionally Low

1. Clinical Channel Framing

Patients receive the device via doctor recommendation, not impulse purchase. Clinical framing creates commitment and proper usage expectations. The OD's authority reduces "buyer's remorse" behavior.

2. Sensory Experience Drives Habit

84% daily usage rate means patients experience tangible benefit within the first week. The thermal therapy creates an immediate sensory response (warmth, relaxation) that reinforces continued use — unlike drops or supplements with delayed/invisible effects.

3. Category Novelty

No comparable product exists in patients' frame of reference. They can't comparison-shop on Amazon. The absence of substitutes eliminates the "I found a cheaper one" return pattern common in commoditized categories.

TriggerPatient dissatisfaction (self-reported)

Tomorrow: Biometric-Verified

TriggerNo measurable improvement after 100 nights
ProofRestScan pre/post comparison (blink rate, fatigue score, HRV)
RiskLower — objective measurement reduces false claims

The strategic play: A biometric-verified guarantee does three things simultaneously. It (1) further reduces return rate by replacing subjective dissatisfaction with objective measurement, (2) generates paired pre/post biometric data that strengthens the clinical evidence base with every guarantee interaction, and (3) creates a marketing story ("we're so confident, we measure the results and guarantee them") that no competitor can match without building their own measurement system.
RestScan-verified guarantee requires: Gen 2 hardware with BLE connectivity, RestScan validation study completion (N=50, 85% Oura correlation target), and privacy policy update to cover biometric data collection. Timeline: post-raise, aligned with Gen 2 launch.
