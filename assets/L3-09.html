Strategic Decision
Umay deploys independent 1099 reps rather than W-2 sales hires for the initial US channel expansion. At $50/unit commission vs. $200K+ OTE for a single FTE, the 1099 model requires 4,000+ units/year per rep to reach cost equivalence. Variable cost structure preserves cash during the scaling phase while leveraging reps' existing ophthalmic relationships. Head of Growth (US) manages the rep network; reps do not replace the hire.

Section 3 · Compliance & Claims Training Requirements

Mandatory Compliance Provisions

Every 1099 rep agreement includes binding compliance clauses that protect Umay's regulatory positioning. Violation of claims governance is grounds for immediate termination.

Clause 1 · Approved Claims Only
Representative shall use only Umay-approved marketing materials and claims language. Representative shall not make any claims regarding disease diagnosis, treatment, cure, or prevention. All product positioning must adhere to Umay's Claims Governance Framework (Tier 1 and Tier 2 claims only).
Clause 2 · Claims Training Certification
Representative must complete Umay's Claims Training Module (60 minutes) within 14 days of agreement execution and annually thereafter. Certification includes: approved language library, prohibited claims list, regulatory context briefing, and scenario-based testing (pass rate: 80%).
Clause 3 · Intellectual Property & Confidentiality
Representative acknowledges all Umay IP (patents, trademarks, trade secrets, customer lists, pricing) remains property of Umay Care Holdings Inc. Representative shall not disclose confidential information to competing entities during or after the term. Clinic relationship data generated during the engagement belongs to Umay.
Clause 4 · Non-Compete (Product Category)
During the term and for 6 months following termination, Representative shall not promote, sell, or distribute any periocular thermal therapy device or at-home dry eye treatment device that directly competes with Umay Rest. This does not restrict sale of non-competing ophthalmic products (frames, contact lenses, supplements, instruments).
Clause 5 · Indemnification & Insurance
Representative maintains independent contractor insurance. Umay's product liability coverage ($2M CGL) covers product-related claims. Representative indemnifies Umay against claims arising from unauthorized representations or unapproved marketing activities.

Why This Matters for Investors
The 1099 rep model is the mechanism that converts Umay from founder-led sales to a repeatable, variable-cost distribution engine. At $50-60/unit, it costs 17-21% of wholesale revenue — comparable to medtech distribution benchmarks (15-25%). The claims training and compliance clauses protect the company's regulatory positioning as it scales beyond founder control.
This template requires legal review before execution. Specific provisions (non-compete enforceability, trailing commission structure, indemnification scope) vary by US state and should be validated by employment counsel in each rep's jurisdiction.
