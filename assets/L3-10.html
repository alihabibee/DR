How HSA/FSA Eligibility Works for Umay

Health Savings Account (HSA)

What it isTax-advantaged savings for medical expenses. Paired with high-deductible health plans.
EligibilityExpenses must be "primarily for medical care" (IRS ¬ß213(d))
Tax benefitPre-tax contribution + tax-free withdrawal for qualified expenses
Umay pathwayLetter of Medical Necessity (LMN) from prescribing OD qualifies the purchase

Flexible Spending Account (FSA)

What it isEmployer-sponsored pre-tax account for medical expenses. Use-it-or-lose-it annual limit.
EligibilitySame IRS ¬ß213(d) "primarily for medical care" standard
Tax benefitPre-tax contribution reduces taxable income
Umay pathwaySame LMN pathway. FSA holders especially motivated to spend before year-end deadline.
