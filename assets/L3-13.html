Why This Is Critical
Cross-border fulfillment from Canada to the US creates duty friction, delivery delays, and unacceptable CAC for US market entry. Establishing a US-based 3PL is a prerequisite for the 1099 rep program, HSA/FSA checkout, and 2-day delivery to US patients. This document maps the transition from the current mixed model to a US drop-ship architecture.

Working Capital: The Sea Freight Float

Why Sea Freight Requires Capital

Sea freight takes 35-45 days door-to-door vs. 7-10 days for air. This means Umay needs to fund ~45 days of inventory in transit before it reaches the US 3PL.
