All IP assigned: Every engineering and design contract includes full IP assignment to Umay Care Holdings Inc.

No exclusivity traps: No agreement gives any partner exclusive rights that would constrain Umay's optionality.

No outstanding litigation: Zero claims, disputes, or threatened actions.
