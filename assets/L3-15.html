Why RestScan's Local-Only Architecture Is a Strategic Advantage

By processing all facial analysis in the browser (MediaPipe JavaScript), RestScan avoids triggering biometric privacy laws (Illinois BIPA, Texas CUBI, Washington state biometric law) that would require explicit consent frameworks, data retention policies, and audit obligations.

No video leaves the device. No face landmarks leave the device. No biometric identifiers are collected, stored, or transmitted by Umay.

This isn't just a privacy decision â€” it's a regulatory moat. Competitors who build server-side facial analysis inherit biometric compliance obligations in every US state where BIPA-equivalent laws exist. Umay sidesteps all of them by architectural choice.
