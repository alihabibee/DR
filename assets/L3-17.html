⚠ April 2026 Debt Cliff

Total monthly debt service resumes at $19,762/month in April 2026 (payment deferrals ending). Combined with $28K monthly burn, post-April cash requirement is ~$48K/month.

BDC GSA is the existential risk: The General Security Agreement on all company assets means BDC can seize inventory, IP, and equipment if payments are missed. $350K of the raise is allocated to debt service and runway buffer specifically to address this.

Mitigation: Bridge funding closes before April. $350K allocation covers 7+ months of debt service, providing runway to revenue-from-backlog (1,600 units × $285 = $456K revenue once shipped).
