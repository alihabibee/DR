Cost Structure Philosophy

Fixed costs stay minimal. Only 3 new FTEs are added with this raise. All field sales are variable cost (1099 reps at $50-60/unit). Engineering remains project-based (Tangent contract). This keeps the burn manageable and preserves optionality.

Post-raise burn of ~$65-75K/month is covered by the $2M raise for 18-24 months even before accounting for revenue from the 1,600-unit backlog ($456K+ at wholesale).

Hiring Sequence Rationale

#1Head of Growth (US): Unblocks the US channel. Can't deploy reps without someone to manage them.
#2Product/Tech Lead: Takes Gen 2 + RestScan off Ali. 2-3 month lag is fine — projects are defined.
#3Ops Manager: Needed when 3PL is live and order volume scales. Can be last because existing ops support holds.
